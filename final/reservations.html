Rental Rules Information

Renter's Information

  • Renter's Information: The rental agreement is between Scoots Scooter Rental Company ("Scoots") and the individual named as the renter on the agreement.
  • Rental Period: The rental period begins upon the issuance of the scooter to the renter and ends at the agreed-upon return time specified in the agreement.
  • Rental Fee: The renter agrees to pay the specified rental fee, which includes the cost of renting the scooter for the duration of the agreed rental period.
  • Security Deposit: A security deposit may be required at the time of rental to cover any potential damages to the scooter. The deposit will be refunded to the renter upon the safe return of the scooter in its original condition.

Rules and Regulations

  • Safety Gear: The renter agrees to wear appropriate safety gear, including a helmet, while operating the scooter
  • Compliance with Laws: The renter agrees to comply with all local traffic laws and regulations while operating the scooter. This includes but is not limited to obeying speed limits, traffic signals, and road signs.
  • Prohibited Activities: The renter agrees not to engage in any reckless or prohibited activities while operating the scooter, including but not limited to: riding under the influence of drugs or alcohol, carrying passengers exceeding the scooter's capacity, or participating in racing or stunt riding.
  • Scooter Maintenance: The renter agrees to maintain the scooter in good condition throughout the rental period. This includes regular checks of tire pressure, oil levels, and any other necessary maintenance tasks as outlined in the provided user manual.
  • Reporting of Incidents: In the event of an accident, theft, or damage to the scooter, the renter agrees to immediately report the incident to Scoots and provide all necessary information, including a police report if applicable.

Liability and Insurance

  • Liability Waiver: The renter acknowledges and agrees that they are responsible for any damages or injuries that occur while operating the scooter during the rental period.
  • Insurance Coverage: Scoots may offer optional insurance coverage for an additional fee to protect against damages to the scooter or third-party liability. The renter is encouraged to inquire about insurance options at the time of rental.

Additional Information

  • Age Requirement: The renter must be of legal age and hold a valid driver's license to operate the scooter
  • Reservation Policy: Reservations for scooter rentals are subject to availability and may require advance booking. Walk-in might rent if we have availability
  • Cancellation Policy: Cancellations made within a specified timeframe may be subject to a cancellation fee.
  • Customer Support: Scoots provides customer support and assistance throughout the rental period. Contact information for emergency assistance is provided on the rental agreement.

By signing this rental agreement, the renter acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agreed to all terms and conditions outlined herein. Failure to comply with the terms of the agreement may result in termination of the rental and forfeiture of any rental fees or security deposit
