La Paz Facts

La Paz holds the title of the highest administrative capital city in the world, sitting at an elevation of over 3,500 meters (11,500 feet) above sea level.

Considering just the core urban area of La Paz, the estimated population sits around 816,044 residents as of 2020. This makes it the third-most populous city in Bolivia.

La Paz is surrounded by the majestic peaks of the Andes, including the iconic Illimani mountain which towers over the city at 6,438 meters (21,122 feet).
